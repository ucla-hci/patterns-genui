# One Sentence
Introduces "curated reasoning" — making LLM reasoning structures inspectable, reorganizable, and selectively regenerable by designers — to align LLM generation with iterative design intent.

---

# More Sentences
DesignerlyLoop addresses the mismatch between design thinking (iterative, exploratory intent) and LLM prompt-response cycles (treats input as complete specification). Designers inspect and manipulate the LLM's intermediate reasoning rather than accepting outputs passively. A study with 20 designers showed significant improvements in design quality and creativity.

---

# Take-Away
- Addresses designer transparency over LLM reasoning, not design pattern conditioning. Supports the same transparency goal as PatternGenUI through a different mechanism (reasoning inspection vs. pattern retrieval). Cite as motivation, not prior art.
